THE CUBE DEMANDS YOUR TIME.

The Cube is an experiment in existence, a relentless trial where time is your only asset.

Every second is a gamble, every action drains your life, and every death resets your reality.

Can you outwit the Cube's illusion of freedom, or will you succumb to its infinite cycle?

FIGHT TO SURVIVE

Engage in fast-paced FPS combat where precision and strategy are paramount. Master weapon handling and tactical positioning to outmaneuver relentless enemies. In the Cube, only the skilled prevail, while the reckless face swift punishment.

NOWHERE IS SAFE

Navigate procedurally generated dimensions, each a labyrinth of sterile corridors and impossible architecture. Every run presents unique challenges, from deadly traps to shifting realities, ensuring no path is ever the same.

OTHERS SUFFER WITH YOU

Connect in the multiplayer hub to trade intel, form fragile alliances, or betray for gain. Trust is a rare commodity in the Cube, where every interaction is a calculated risk that could cost you everything.

SACRIFICE FOR STRENGTH

Customize your arsenal with time-infused modifications, trading precious minutes for devastating power. Each upgrade is a gamble: stronger weapons may prolong survival, but at the cost of your dwindling time.

CHOOSE YOUR TORMENT

Accept missions from enigmatic entities, offering time rewards but escalating dangers. Bet on your performance, knowing that overconfidence carries a steep price and failure resets your existence.
